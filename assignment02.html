PRIVACY POLICY
We look forward to welcoming you back to the California Science Center! We are all about stimulating curiosity and inspiring science learning in everyone. To protect vulnerable community members, including our younger guests who are not yet eligible for vaccination against COVID-19, masks are required for EVERYONE ages 2 and up when visiting the California Science Center. Timed-entry reservations are also required.

OUR MISSION #

The California Science Center aspires to stimulate curiosity and inspire science learning in everyone by creating fun, memorable experiences, because we value science as an indispensable tool for understanding our world, accessibility and inclusiveness, and enriching people’s lives.
